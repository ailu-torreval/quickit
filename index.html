How can Quickit help my Business?

Increase Additional Sales

Send offers to your guests through the app, and remind them which cake is good for the coffee before the guest pays his bill.

Access to Data

Get to know your guest better by accessing a lot of consumer data that Quickit collects.

More time for Service

Automating some of the waiters' tasks gives the waiters more time to have fun with the guests and make a profit, even when they are busy.

Overview of Allergens

In the app, the guest has the option to sort the dishes by allergens.

Easy to Order and Pay

During the entire guest's visit, it is possible to order, even when the waiters are busy.

Reduce bacterial Spread

Prevent bacterial spread with Quickit. By avoiding sharing menus and Dankort terminals with guests, you significantly protect yourself and your guests against the spread of bacteria.
